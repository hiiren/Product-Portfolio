# Product-Portfolio

Product Requirement Documents (PRDs) are strategic blueprints that define what to build, why to build it, and how to measure success. Each PRD in this repository follows a structured 10-section framework — executive summary, problem statement, user research, market analysis, competitive analysis, solution overview, RICE prioritization, success metrics, roadmap, and impact estimation.

This repository is a collection of PRDs authored as part of product management practice at AIAvenue Agency. It covers 10 real-world product concepts spanning AI SaaS, fintech, edtech, compliance, automation, and UX design. Each PRD reflects end-to-end product thinking — from market sizing and competitive positioning to feature prioritization and go-to-market planning.

## Product Requirement Documents

### AI Marketing Compliance Platform
- Identified 3–5 day compliance review cycles and regulatory penalty exposure as core pain points in insurance marketing
- Defined an AI-powered compliance scanning solution with 12-rule IRDAI/brand/legal engine and bounding-box violation overlays
- Conducted user segmentation (Marketing Manager, Compliance Officer, CMO) and TAM–SAM–SOM sizing (₹120 Cr / ₹18 Cr / ₹4.5 Cr)
- Mapped product features to KPIs such as cycle time reduction (3.8 days → 4 hours) and violation detection rate (92% → 99.5%)

### Laundry POS & Management System
- Diagnosed operational fragmentation in Indian laundry businesses — manual order entry, no inventory tracking, no CRM
- Designed an AI-powered POS with 4-step order wizard, Morning Briefing, Agentic Chat POS, and Claude Vision stain detection
- Applied RICE prioritization to balance AI features against core POS functionality for MVP
- Linked feature outcomes to order processing speed, customer retention, and revenue per ticket

### Live Stock & Options Trading Scanner
- Identified a gap in real-time NSE F&O analysis tools for retail traders — existing scanners lack AI sentiment and multi-factor filtering
- Built a 7-filter AI pipeline scanning all 188 NSE F&O equities with real-time OI tracking and breakout detection
- Analyzed retail trading pain points: information overload, delayed signals, and lack of actionable alerts
- Estimated impact on trade decision speed, signal accuracy, and user daily active usage

### AI-Powered Astrology Dashboard
- Framed a product gap between traditional astrology (static, text-heavy kundali PDFs) and modern AI-driven interactive experiences
- Designed a Vedic Kundali and Lal Kitab analysis platform with AI predictions, Dasha timelines, and 60+ remedy engine
- Applied NASA JPL ephemeris data for transit chart accuracy — combining ancient knowledge with scientific precision
- Prioritized features using RICE: interactive Q&A, transit charts, and remedy recommendations as highest-impact modules

### Trading Education Landing Page
- Identified lead generation as the primary conversion challenge for a trading education business
- Designed a high-conversion landing page with email OTP verification via Firebase and two-tier pricing model
- Conducted competitive analysis of fintech education landing pages to identify best-in-class conversion patterns
- Linked UX decisions to lead capture rate, OTP verification completion, and paid conversion metrics

### Field Service Management Platform
- Analyzed enterprise field service pain points: manual dispatch, no real-time tracking, and disconnected Shopify order pipeline
- Designed a Kanban-based dispatch system with Shopify webhook integration, Cognito auth, and AppSync GraphQL
- Segmented users into Admin and Technician roles with distinct workflows and access controls
- Estimated impact on dispatch time, technician utilization, and order-to-service completion cycle

### SaaS Dashboard UX Redesign
- Evaluated the existing SaaS dashboard through usability heuristics — identified high cognitive load and poor mobile responsiveness
- Redesigned a 6-screen mobile journey with conversational AI onboarding, Business Chat, and progress tracking
- Applied UX research methods: heuristic evaluation, user flow mapping, and competitive benchmarking
- Connected UX improvements to activation rate, feature discovery, and Day-7 retention

### EdTech Student Communication Platform
- Identified fragmentation across WhatsApp, Telegram, and LinkedIn as a core productivity drain for edtech students
- Designed a unified 5-feature platform: Cohort Chat, AI Doubt Feed, Peer Profiles, AI Study Rooms, and Alumni Bridge
- Conducted market sizing (TAM–SAM–SOM) in the Indian edtech communication tools segment
- Prioritized features using RICE with AI Doubt Feed and Cohort Chat scoring highest for reach and impact

### Compliance QA Simulation Tool
- Framed a zero-dependency browser prototype to demonstrate AI compliance scanning concepts before full-stack investment
- Built a simulation with dashboard, checklist builder, progress animation, and audit log — all in vanilla HTML/CSS/JS
- Used the prototype for stakeholder buy-in and user acceptance testing before committing to full development
- Linked prototype success metrics to stakeholder confidence score and feedback incorporation rate

### Business Process Automation (n8n)
- Identified repetitive manual workflows (stock alerts, email processing, CRM updates) as time sinks for small businesses
- Designed n8n-based automation pipelines: Telegram bots for stock analysis, daily news digests, and email auto-reply with notes
- Analyzed cost-benefit of automation vs manual effort across three client scenarios
- Estimated ROI in hours saved per week, error reduction, and response time improvement
